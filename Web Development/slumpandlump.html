The Aftermath

We continue this multi-part story to see how Catto processes his owner's actions.

Catto hops up onto the table and proceeds to make it his own domain by becoming a blob on Greg's work. "Aw, Catto. I'm a little busy, but that's adorable. Here's some tummy rubs." Satisfied, Catto retracts his statement saying: "Greg has earned my respect back by doing the universal act of friendship of feline and human by scriting my belly."
